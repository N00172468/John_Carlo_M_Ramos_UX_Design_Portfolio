About

John Carlo M. Ramos

Born and raised in The Philippines but grew up here in Dublin, Ireland.

The designer in me was calling before I even knew it. Back in secondary school, my top and favourite subjects were construction studies, D.C.G and English. The reason being is that I'm good at making things with my hands, I enjoy solving real-world problems and I love to know and help people.

After graduating from secondary school, I became a student in Product Design at I.T Carlow. That was when I figured that I had "design" in me. That was when I fell in love with designing.

After a year of being drenched with the fundamentals of design, I decided to move back and study at Dublin. To be perfectly honest, the only reason why I chose Creative Computing in I.A.D.T was because I read the word “design” in their description. Yes I learned how to code front-end & back-end but what I was craving for was the design thinking, design methodologies, user research and essentially anything to do with design. Luckily, I came to the right place.

Four years later, my passion for design did nothing but grew. I am nothing but more determined, more excited and more hungry to become a UI/UX designer.

Services

What I Do.

A student with the passion, admiration and curiosity for design. Dedicated to use the designer’s mentality to solve problems and conceptualize innovative ideas.

  • UI/UX Design

    This includes creativity, design thinking, conceptualization, problem solving and interaction & user-centred research. Projects have been focused on the users’ needs, design process, visual hierarchy, consistency, accessibility, usability, typography, visual grammar and narrative design.

  • Visual Design

    This includes InVision Studio, Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere, Autodesk Sketchbook, draw.io and many other similar applications. These are used to iterate wireframes, concepts, ERDs and U.M.L diagrams as well as post processing photographs and videos.

  • Web Design

    This includes HTML5, CSS, SCSS and JavaScript as well as frameworks and component libraries such as React, Vue, Bootstrap, Material UI and IBM Carbon.
